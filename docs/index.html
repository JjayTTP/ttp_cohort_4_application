About me

I am a current sophomore at Macaulay Honors College at John Jay. I am currently double majoring in computer science and information security (CSIS) and philosophy. I have experience with Python, C++, HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Since I participated in a Model United Nations committee in which the topic was legal liability of artificial intelligence during my junior year of high school, I knew I wanted to study computer science and go into the field of cybersecurity. I declared my second major in philosophy in Feburary 2023 so I can better understand the moral and legal aspect of computer science and work to create and ensure ethical, safe, and secure technology. I am very interested in the intersection of philosophy and computer science, specifically symbolic logic, computational philosophy, and ethical/legal issues regarding AI.
